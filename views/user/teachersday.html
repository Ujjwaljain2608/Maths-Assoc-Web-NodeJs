Teachers' Day Celebration

“The light of the world, the beacon in the dark, and the hope that gives us the strength to survive is our teacher.”


The very first event of the previous semester started with the Teacher’s Day Celebration. On this glorious occasion, the members of the Mathematics association took the opportunity to convey our gratitude to all our teachers, who have had a great hand in shaping us. The aim of the event was to have an interactive and friendly session with the professors. We had a vote of thanks for all our Professors, thanking them for their immense support in our lives. The event was followed by a few words of wisdom from some of the faculty members. The event included a Song quiz competition which was played among three teams of Professors, Ph.D. scholars, and students, all of whom performed very well and had a great time. We also got an opportunity to cherish some great memories with our professors’ fun side. There was no doubt that the competition winners of the quiz was the professor’s team. The event was taken very sportingly by all the faculty members. This event was followed by snacks and drinks. The overall celebration was very lively and joyful. During the event a different kind of bond and atmosphere was formed between the students and faculty members. The event was enjoyed much by the faculty members and the students alike.
